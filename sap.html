What is SAP?

The Backbone of Global
Enterprise Business

SAP — Systems, Applications & Products in Data Processing — is the world's leading ERP software. It manages business operations for 77% of the Fortune 500 across 180+ countries, handling trillions in daily transactions. SAP professionals are never out of demand.

01 / ABAP
Technical Programming
ABAP is SAP's proprietary programming language. Used to build custom reports, forms, interfaces, enhancements, BAPIs, BDCs & S/4HANA apps. It's the most in-demand SAP technical skill — and our core speciality at SAPZCODES.
02 / FICO
Finance & Controlling
The most widely implemented SAP module. FICO covers the entire financial operations of an organisation — from journal entries to management reporting. Consistently the #1 most hired SAP functional role.
03 / S/4HANA
Next-Generation ERP
SAP S/4HANA is the intelligent ERP built on in-memory computing. Every major company is migrating right now — creating a massive talent shortage and skyrocketing demand for S/4HANA consultants.
